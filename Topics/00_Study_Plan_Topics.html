💡 Study Tips & Daily Routine

⏱ Per Topic (~85 min)

Read summary (20 min) → Flashcards until confident (30 min) → Take quiz (15 min) → Review wrong answers (20 min)

🏖 Weekend Days (3h)

Don't learn new material. Use the time to review completed topics with flashcards and self-quiz. Consolidate rather than push forward.

🔑 Highest Priority

Module 4 (19%) + Module 3 (17%) + Module 6 (17%) = 53% of the exam. Make sure you know these cold before moving to lower-weight modules.

🃏 Flashcard Strategy

Go through all cards once. Mark ones you get wrong. Repeat the marked ones. Don't move on until you can flip through the full deck without errors.

📝 Quiz Strategy

The CSCP uses scenario-based questions. After each quiz, don't just check the score — read every explanation, even for correct answers.

🔄 Day 13 (Aug 1)

Finish Module 8. Then do a final pass on the highest-weight modules (M4, M3, M6). Focus on definitions and ASCM-specific terminology.
